Pharmaceutical Data

Access pharmaceutical data, systems and dashboards from the NHS Business Services Authority. This area provides information on prescribing patterns, costs, and trends across primary care in England.

Key Systems

ePACT2

Electronic Prescribing and Cost Tool (ePACT2) provides access to prescription data at NHS organization and prescriber level.

Use ePACT2 to:

  • Monitor prescribing patterns
  • Identify cost-saving opportunities
  • Compare prescribing across organizations
Request Access

Manage Your Service (MYS)

MYS allows pharmacy contractors to manage their NHS services and submit claims for payment.

Use MYS to:

  • Submit monthly prescription declarations
  • Claim for advanced services
  • Update pharmacy details
Request Access

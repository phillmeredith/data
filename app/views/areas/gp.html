General Practice (GP) Data

Access General Practice data, systems and dashboards from the NHS Business Services Authority. This area provides information on GP prescribing, patient registrations, and practice performance across England.

Key Systems

GP Prescribing Dashboard

Access detailed prescribing data at GP practice level, including cost and volume analysis.

Use this system to:

  • Compare prescribing patterns across practices
  • Identify outliers in prescribing behavior
  • Monitor adherence to local formularies
Request Access

GP Registration System

View and analyze patient registration data across GP practices.

Use this system to:

  • Monitor patient list sizes
  • Track registration changes over time
  • Analyze demographic patterns

Use Case Examples

Improving Diabetes Management

An Integrated Care System used GP prescribing data to identify practices with lower than expected prescribing of newer diabetes medications, leading to targeted education and a 22% improvement in appropriate prescribing.

Workforce Planning

NHS England used GP demographic data to model future workforce requirements, helping to target recruitment initiatives in areas with aging GP populations.
